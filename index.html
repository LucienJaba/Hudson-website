Building runs in the family. My great-grandfather was a developer, my grandfather a builder, my stepfather a builder — and Hudson, the name on this company, is the middle name passed down through the line. It's mine too.

My grandfather is also the one who gave me the nickname “Goose.” So when we say the goose is loose, it's not a gimmick — it's four generations of building showing up in Teton Valley to do honest work.

I spent 12+ years running a painting and home-improvement business before bringing it here. My father was an immigrant from Nicaragua who built his own company from nothing — that work ethic is half of who I am, and it's in everything we touch.
